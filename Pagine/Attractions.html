Duomo di Milano

A magnificent Gothic cathedral and the symbol of Milan, known for its stunning architecture and panoramic views from the terrace.

Galleria Vittorio Emanuele II

One of the world's oldest shopping malls, this elegant gallery is a hub for high-end shopping, dining, and socializing.

Leonardo da Vinci's Last Supper

Housed in the Convent of Santa Maria delle Grazie, this iconic mural painting by Leonardo da Vinci is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Sforza Castle

A historic fortress that now hosts several museums and art collections, including works by Michelangelo and Leonardo da Vinci.

Brera District

Known for its bohemian atmosphere, Brera is a charming neighborhood filled with art galleries, boutiques, and vibrant cafes.

Pinacoteca di Brera

An art gallery located in the Brera district, housing an impressive collection of Italian Renaissance art..

La Scala Opera House

One of the world's most renowned opera houses, La Scala has a rich history and continues to host world-class performances.

Civic Aquarium of Milan

A historic aquarium located in Sempione Park, featuring a variety of marine life and educational exhibits.

Navigli District

Famous for its picturesque canals, Navigli is a trendy area lined with cafes, shops, and a lively nightlife scene.

San Siro Stadium

One of Europe's most famous football stadiums, home to both A.C. Milan and Inter Milan, offering guided tours for sports enthusiasts.

Biblioteca Ambrosiana

Founded by Cardinal Federico Borromeo in the early 17th century, this historic library and gallery house an extensive collection of manuscripts.

Bosco Verticale

A modern architectural marvel, the Bosco Verticale consists of two residential towers covered with more than 9,000 trees.

Information

The Milan presentation website is a comprehensive informational portal dedicated to the lively Italian city. It provides users with a detailed overview of the unique features and attractions of Milan. Through an intuitive navigation system, the site offers updated information on cultural, gastronomic, and artistic events, allowing users to discover and participate in the experiences that the city has to offer. Thanks to its well-organized structure and thematic resources, the website serves as a reliable guide for residents and visitors, facilitating the exploration and understanding of Milan's vibrant culture.
